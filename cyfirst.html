WHITE SAUCE PASTA

Pasta with White Sauce is an easy recipe for pasta cooked in a delicious white sauce, also known as b√©chamel. Simply prepare the white sauce, a mixture of butter, milk, flour and spices, and pour it over your cooked pasta.

This popular dish is vegetarian and can be served as a snack or light dinner. Make it more filling and nutritious by adding some mixed greens

When preparing pasta recipes, whole wheat pasta is always a great, healthy option. I used whole wheat penne in this recipe, but you can use any pasta you prefer, including different types like macaroni, rigatoni, or farfalle.

Ingrediants for making white mayonnaise

  • salt

Steps to follow for making White Sauce pasta

  1. place pan on stove
  2. Add Oil - 1 Tsp
  3. Add Butter - 2 TSP
  4. Add Carrot,boiled sweet corns and capsicum-1/4 each
  5. Add maida - 2 Tsp
  6. Add the prepared milk in batches and stir continuously to avoid any lumps
  7. Add Oregano - 2 Tsp
  8. Add chilli flakes - 1/2 Tsp
  9. Add Black pepper powder - 1/2 Tsp
  10. Add salt to taste
  11. Add Boiled pasta
  12. Add processed cheese -2 slices(Optional)
